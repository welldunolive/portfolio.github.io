About

Greetings! I am a Data Science Enthusiast from Long Island, NY specializing in Python. With an Applied Mathematics and Statistics degree, I've always had a passion for numbers and large data. Coding has always been a fear of mine, but with amazing instructors from General Assembly, I slowly overcame my fear and even found myself enjoying it.

My path towards data started while I was working in a corporate position. The work was dull and unrelated to my field, but one thing I enjoyed was dealing with large datasets. However Excel was not enough for me; I wanted to do more. I taught myself basic SQL then moved onto Python to start comprehensively analyzing larger datasets. I quickly signed up for General Assembly's Data Science Immersive Program to further my knowledge. Most of my skills were learned and polished from the program.

Aside from my technical skills, I love to cook and build computers. Most of my day is spent on my computer or in my kitchen.
